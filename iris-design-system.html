Overview · Design Principles

Design Principles

Five rules that govern every IRIS interface. These are not suggestions — they are the contract between the brand and the people who use it.

Space = Clarity

Generous whitespace is not decoration — it is the visual proof of the brand promise. Never crowd.

Functional & Neutral

Terse, legible, score-friendly UI copy. Serve analysts and risk officers who distrust hype.

Azure Drives Actions

#41A9F6 for all interactive states: buttons, links, active nav, focus rings.

Semantic Risk Colors Only

Red, amber, and green are reserved for risk data only. Never use them as brand decoration.

Tabular Numbers Everywhere

font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums on all scores, percentiles, and table figures.
